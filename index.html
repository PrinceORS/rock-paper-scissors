ROCK, PAPER, SCISSORS

Rock paper scissors is usually a hand game, played between two people, in which each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with an outstretched hand.

These shapes are "rock", "paper", and "scissors".

In the computer version we'll enter our choice by typing either 'Rock', 'Paper' or 'Scissors'

The computer will make a random choice.

The rules are:
Rock beats scissors but loses to paper.
Scissors beat paper but loses to rock.
Paper beats Rock but loses to Scissors.
